Why Founders & Coders?

Founders and coders offers peer learning, which is what I enjoy the most when it comes to learning as it allows you to be able to get know people and work with them as you would in the tech industry itself. Another important factor about why I want to apply to founders and coders is also the range of diversity and inclusivity. This is really important to me.
